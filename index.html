Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I merge multiple PDFs, DOCX, and JPGs together?

Our PDF Merger supports instant on-the-fly client-side conversion. Simply drop mixed files (images like JPG/PNG, text sheets like DOCX, or direct PDFs) into the drag-drop bucket. The app automatically extracts elements and parses them page-by-page into a unified vector PDF.

Are my documents secure? Does PDFFlow upload files?

Zero files ever hit our servers. All conversion engines, including DocxReader and canvas compressors, run inside your native browser runtime. You can even disconnect your internet entirely after launching the applet, and all functions will work 100% offline.

How does the PDF splitter extract pages?

Under the Split segment, load your document and select whether you want to download a zip bundle of separate pages or extract unique ranges (e.g., Pages 1, 3-5). The underlying script parses selected page ranges and constructs a new optimized PDF container on-the-fly.

Does compression affect the vector quality of PDF text?

No! Our compression algorithm isolates high-density raster elements (like embedded images or screenshots) to optimize them, while carefully maintaining PDF structural text pathways, vector graphs, and font shapes at maximum crispness.
